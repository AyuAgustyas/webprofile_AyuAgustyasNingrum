ABOUT

I am an active fifth-semester student at Trunojoyo Madura University, majoring in Informatics Education. I come from Ponorogo, a city famous for the traditional art of Reog Ponorogo. As the third child among three siblings, I grew up in a family of farmers, which instilled in me the values of hard work and resilience from an early age. Continuing my education to the current level has been a challenging journey, but my determination and strong desire to learn have kept me going. I have a dream to change not only my own future but also that of my family through education. Additionally, I have a great interest in outdoor activities, and playing in the great outdoors is something that brings me immense joy.


Visi :


"My life's vision is to create a better world by contributing to the development of a just and sustainable society while achieving balance and personal happiness in my own life."



Misi :


"My life's mission is to work diligently in my education and career to acquire the knowledge and skills necessary to contribute to community development. I will also consistently prioritize a balanced life, maintaining my physical and mental health, as well as nurturing strong relationships with family and friends."
